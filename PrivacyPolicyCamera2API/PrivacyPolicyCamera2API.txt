Privacy Policy for Camera2API
==============================
This Application asks for the following permissions:

android.permission.CAMERA
android.permission.WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE
android.permission.READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE
android.permission.RECORD_AUDIO

Installing the Camera2 API Application You are agreed to provide Access to your Device Camera,File System, Audio recording for Application functioning . This access is limited to user only. None of the data present at your device is shared with us.
